1. Click “New Claim” on the home screen.
2. Fill in your personal and insurance details in the form shown.

4. Review your information.
5. Click “Submit”.

### Tracking Your Claim

1. Go to “Track Claim” on the home screen.
2. Enter your confirmation number and your email.
3. View the current status and any messages from your insurer.

Updates happen automatically when your claim moves forward.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting and Tips

- If the program does not open in your browser, check your internet connection.
- Make sure no other programs block ports on your computer; InsureEase uses default web ports.
- Clear your browser cache if pages seem outdated.
- Use the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge for best results.
- If files do not unzip, try a different extractor program.
- Keep your confirmation number and email used for registration secure.
